# Review Packets

Review packets organize evidence and draft outputs for human review before AAB publication or standards reuse.

## Packet Contents

- Draft output or research question.
- Evidence table.
- Source links and registry/export IDs.
- Claim-to-source trace.
- Limitations and uncertainty notes.
- Reviewer questions.
- Publication or reuse recommendation.

## Review Outcomes

- `accept`: ready for publication or reuse.
- `revise`: needs changes before publication or reuse.
- `defer`: valid topic but needs more evidence or governance decision.
- `reject`: not suitable for AAB use, with rationale.
