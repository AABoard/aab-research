# Contributing to AAB Research

This repository supports public, source-traced research collaboration for AAB. Contributions should improve evidence quality, research transparency, literature synthesis, methods clarity, or publication readiness.

## Contribution Types

- Research brief proposals.
- Literature review or annotated bibliography additions.
- Evidence table updates.
- Framework crosswalk research.
- Consensus report support materials.
- Methods and source-quality rubric improvements.
- Review packet preparation.
- Corrections to published or draft research claims.

## Submission Expectations

A useful research contribution should include:

- Research question or topic.
- Relevant learner context, grade band, institution type, or policy context.
- Public source links or AAB registry record references.
- Clear distinction between evidence and interpretation.
- Known limitations or uncertainty.
- Proposed output type, such as research brief, working paper, crosswalk, or evidence table.

## Do Not Submit

- Private student data.
- Confidential partner, school, pilot, donor, or personnel documents.
- MongoDB credentials or private connection strings.
- AI-generated summaries without source links or human review notes.
- Product endorsements disguised as research conclusions.

## Pull Request Checklist

- [ ] The research question or claim is clear.
- [ ] Sources are cited or registry/export IDs are referenced.
- [ ] Limitations and uncertainty are included.
- [ ] Private data and credentials are excluded.
- [ ] Claims do not imply AAB endorsement unless approved through the relevant review process.
- [ ] Related standards, data, or governance impacts are noted.
